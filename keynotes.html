Biography

Dr.Alok Naugarhiya

Assistant Professor

Department of Electronics and communication Engineering, NIT Raipur.

BE, M.Tech (MNNIT Allahabad), Ph.D (IIIT Jabalpur)

Member: - IEEE Senior, IETE, IET

Dr. Alok Naugarhiya is associated with Department of Electronics and Communication at NIT Raipur. Dr. Alok has supervised six doctoral theses and sixteen M.Tech theses in the field of Semiconductor devices and VLSI. He has been taking care for various administrative responsibilities like; he is the In-charge of VLSI and Microelectronics lab, coordinator of the M. Tech. Program, departmental T&P coordinator, and External training coordinator of NIT Raipur. He has published 90 research articles in national and international journal/conference. He is also serving as chair for various workshop and conferences.
