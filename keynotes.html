2 Dr. B. V. Reddy

Department of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering Ontario Tech University, Canada.

Biomass Combustion and Gasification, Fluidized Bed Combustors, Combined Cycle Power Generation and Solar Energy.

Dr. Bale Viswanadha Reddy (B.V. Reddy) is a Professor in Mechanical Engineering in Faculty of Engineering and Applied Science, University of Ontario Institute of Technology (UOIT), Oshawa, Ontario, Canada. Prior to this Dr. Reddy also worked as an Associate Professor in Mechanical Engineering Department, University of New Brunswick (UNB), Fredericton, Canada. Dr. Reddy received his MTech and PhD in Mechanical Engineering from IIT, Kharagpur, India. Dr. Reddy research interests are in the area of advanced and sustainable energy systems, exergy analysis, energy management, waste heat recovery and alternative energy sources. He has led funded research projects in the area of energy systems. Dr. Reddy has published 160 papers in refereed journals and refereed conference proceedings. He is also involved in the organization of many international conferences as a chair, track chair, organizing committee member, advisory committee member etc. He has also chaired technical sessions in international conferences and has delivered key note and invited presentations in conferences. Dr. Reddy also contributed book chapters along with his research collaborators in energy systems area. Dr. Reddy has also received best professor award for teaching excellence five times both in India (VIT, Vellore) and in Canada (UNB, Fredericton; UOIT, Oshawa).

Biography

3 Dr. Vishal Chettry, Assistant Professor

Department of Architecture, Planning and Design IIT BHU, Varanasi.

Urban Sprawl, Bioclimatic Architecture, Urban Heat Island, Healthy Community

2 Dr. Vishal Chettry, Assistant Professor Department of Architecture, Planning and Design IIT BHU, Varanasi Urban Sprawl, Bioclimatic Architecture, Urban Heat Island, Healthy Community Dr. Vishal Chettry is working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Architecture, Planning and Design, Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi. He was awarded a Ph.D. from VNIT Nagpur and had earlier worked as an Assistant Professor at Manipal School of Architecture & Planning (MSAP), MAHE, Manipal, India and Poornima University, Jaipur. He was awarded a Master's Degree in Urban and Regional Planning from the School of Planning and Architecture (SPA) Bhopal and a Bachelor's Degree in Architecture from MIET Gondia, affiliated with Nagpur University (2009-2014). He is an editorial board member of the journal "Discover Sustainability" indexed by Scopus and Web of Science. He has organized international conferences, workshops and training programs in the past. He won a certificate of appreciation for the best thesis project on the "City Center at Guwahati" from the IIA Nagpur chapter in 2014. He has also won a certificate of appreciation from Poornima University Jaipur for his academic contribution during 2016-2017. He has published journal papers indexed in SCIE/ Scopus, presented research papers at renowned International and National conferences, and participated in various workshops. Dr. Chettry has a strong research background and interest in various areas of urban studies. His research interests include urban sprawl, vernacular architecture, heritage studies, healthy communities, urban sustainability, urban heat island, and the application of RS and GIS in the urban and regional planning domain.

4 Dr. Pradeep Singh

Associate Professor

Department of Computer Sc. & Engineering National Institute of Technology Raipur

Artificial Intelligence

Dr. Pradeep Singh is currently the Head of the Department of Computer Science Engineering at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Raipur, India. He obtained his M.Tech. degree from the Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, India, and his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Raipur, India. Dr. Singh has published over 100 research articles in peer-reviewed journals and international conferences. He is a regular reviewer for several prestigious journals, including IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, Knowledge-Based Systems, and IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence. His research interests encompass Computational Intelligence, Software Defect Prediction, and Deep Learning. Dr. Singh is a Senior Member of the IEEE and was recognized as one of the World's Top 2% Researchers and Influential Scientists in 2023 by Stanford University in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Image Processing (The list was prepared by Scientists from Stanford University USA & Published by Elsevier).

Dr. Ashish Nayyar

Professor

Department of Mechanical Engineering Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Jaipur.

Biodiesel, Alternative fuels

Prof. (Dr.) Ashish Nayyar acquired his Ph.D. (Alternative Fuels) and M.Tech. (Energy Engineering) from MNIT, Jaipur. He has done his BE in Mechanical Engineering from M.B.M. Engineering College, Jodhpur. He has 22 years of teaching and research experience in engineering field. Presently he is working as Professor in Department of Mechanical Engineering, Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management and Gramothan, Jaipur. Prof. Ashish Nayyar has published several papers in reputed national and international journals. He is a member of SAEINDIA, Institution of Engineers (India), ISTE and reviewer of many reputed journals. He was awarded with Travel Grant from AICTE, New Delhi and Department of Science and Technology, Rajasthan, India for participation in the international conference at NC, USA in 2017. He has organized various National and International conferences. He has also received grants from Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, India & Science and Engineering Research Board, India and AICTE (New Delhi) under various schemes. Guided 20 M.Tech dissertations and many B.Tech. Projects. Guiding Ph.D. thesis of other universities, consultancy to other universities and industries for projects.
