Submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit their papers through sresth.btirt@gmail.com. The file size of a full paper is limited to 100 MB, including figures and references, with a limit of preferably six pages in length (though up to ten pages are acceptable). The papers will undergo a peer-review process.
